 	<p style="hp">Rivers are here mainly to get in your way. They are expensive to bridge, dig and demolish. Their only use is that ports must be connected to rivers.</p>
 	<p style="hp">There are two types of water. Lakes are water that is <b>not</b> connected to the river system; rivers are water that <b>is</b> connected to the river system.
